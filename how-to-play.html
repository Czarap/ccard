How to Play

1. Choose a Category

  • Select from Friends, Romance, or Emotions
  • Each category has unique questions designed for different situations
  • Try the Random option to mix things up

2. Draw a Card

  • Click to reveal your question
  • Take a moment to reflect on the question
  • Share your thoughts openly and honestly

3. Keep the Conversation Flowing

  • Listen actively to others' responses
  • Ask follow-up questions
  • Share your own experiences

4. Draw Another or Switch Categories

  • Keep drawing cards to explore more questions
  • Switch categories to change the conversation direction
  • Take your time with each question
